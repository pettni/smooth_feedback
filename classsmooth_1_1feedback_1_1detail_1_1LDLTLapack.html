Factorize symmetric \( A \) to enable solving \( A x = b \).

A is factorized as \( A = U D U^T \) where \( U \) is upper triangular and \( D \) is block-diagonal.

Parameters
Asymmetric matrix to factorize.
Note
Only the upper triangular part of \( A \) is accessed.
