Factorize symmetric \( A \) to enable solving \( A x = b \).

A is factorized as \( A = P^T L D L^T P \) where \( P \) is a permutation matrix, \( L \) is lower triangular and \( D \) is diagonal.

The permutation matrix is obtained from the fillin-reducing approximate minimum degree (AMD) ordering.

Parameters
Asparse symmetric matrix to factorize in column-major format.
Note
Only the upper triangular part of \( A \) is accessed.
