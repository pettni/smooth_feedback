Detailed Description

template<LieGroup G, Manifold U>
struct smooth::feedback::OptimalControlProblem< G, U >

Optimal control problem defintiion.

Template Parameters
GState space Lie group.
UInput space Lie group.

The optimal control problem is

\[ \begin{cases} \min_{u(\cdot)} & \int_{0}^T \left( (g(t) \ominus g_{des}(t))^T Q (g(t) \ominus g_{des}(t))^T + (u(t) \ominus u_{des}(t))^T R (u(t) \ominus u_{des}(t))^T \right) + (g(T) \ominus g_{des}(T))^T Q_T (g(T) \ominus g_{des}(T)) \\ \text{s.t.} & g(0) = x_0, \\ & g(t) \ominus g_{min} \geq 0, \\ & g_{max} \ominus g(t) \geq 0, \\ & u(t) \ominus u_{min} \geq 0, \\ & u_{max} \ominus u(t) \geq 0, \end{cases} \]

where the cost matrices must be positive semi-definite.
