Detailed Description

template<LieGroup X>
struct smooth::feedback::detail::MPCObj< X >

MPC cost function.

The cost is

\[ \theta(t_f, x_0, x_f, q) = q + (x_f - x_{f, des})^T Q_T (x_f - x_{f, des}). \]

@warn The functor members are only valid at the linearization point \( x_f = x_{f_des} \).
