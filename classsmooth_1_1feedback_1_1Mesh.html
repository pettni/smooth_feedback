Detailed Description

Collocation mesh of interval [0, 1].

[0, 1] is divided into non-overlapping intervals I_i, and each interval I_i has K_i LGR collocation points.

Interval differentiation matrix w.r.t. [0, 1] timescale.

Matrix K+1 x K matrix D s.t.

\[ \begin{bmatrix} y'(\tau_{i, 0}) & y'(\tau_{i, 1}) & \cdots & y'(\tau_{i, K-1}) \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} y_{i, 0} & y_{i, 1} & \cdots & y_{i, K} \end{bmatrix} D \]

, where \( y_{i, j} \in \mathbb{R}^{d \times 1} \) is the function value at scaled time point \( \tau_{i, j} \).
